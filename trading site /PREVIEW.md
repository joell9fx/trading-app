## 🎨 **Design Features**

### **Color Scheme:**
- **Primary**: Blue (#3B82F6) - Trust, professionalism
- **Success**: Green (#22C55E) - Profits, growth
- **Warning**: Orange (#F59E0B) - Caution, alerts
- **Danger**: Red (#EF4444) - Losses, errors
- **Dark Mode**: Deep grays for reduced eye strain

### **Typography:**
- **Font**: Inter - Clean, modern, highly readable
- **Headings**: Bold weights for hierarchy
- **Body**: Regular weight for content
- **Code**: JetBrains Mono for technical content

### **Animations:**
- **Page Transitions**: Smooth fade-ins and slide-ups
- **Hover Effects**: Subtle scaling and color changes
- **Loading States**: Skeleton screens and spinners
- **Micro-interactions**: Button presses and form feedback

### **Responsive Design:**
- **Desktop**: Full sidebar navigation
- **Tablet**: Collapsible sidebar
- **Mobile**: Bottom navigation bar
- **Touch-friendly**: Large buttons and touch targets
